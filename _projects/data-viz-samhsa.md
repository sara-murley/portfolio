Georgetown University | PPOL 5202 Data Visualization for Data Science | September 2025 - December 2025

Developed interactive dashboards and visualizations analyzing access to treatment for substance use disorders using SAMHSA’s TEDS and N-SUMHSS datasets. Work highlighted geographic variation, demographic disparities, and system-level bottlenecks, enabling policymakers to target underserved populations, optimize facility placement, and improve care pathways.
  
**Methods:** Data analysis, interactive dashboards  
**Tools:** R, Shiny, Tableau  
**Data:** SAMHSA TEDS (2006–2023), N-SUMHSS (2023)
